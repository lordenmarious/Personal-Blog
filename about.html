About Me

Hello! I'm Ertuğrul Enis Can, an enthusiastic English language teacher with a passion for learning, creativity, and connection. My teaching journey has been driven by a commitment to helping students unlock their potential through communication-focused methods and innovative technology.

My Passions

Beyond the classroom, I find joy in exploring hobbies that keep me inspired and energized. Video games are one of my favorite pastimes, offering an immersive way to experience new worlds and challenges. Whether it's strategizing in Cities Skylines or diving into epic narratives like The Witcher 3, gaming fuels my curiosity and creativity.

Music is another essential part of my life. I enjoy a variety of genres, with Turkish rap and albums like Kayra & Farazi - Hayalet Islığı holding a special place in my playlist. Music helps me focus while reading, unwind after a busy day, or set the mood for new ideas.

Teaching, learning, gaming, and music are all integral parts of who I am, and they shape the way I connect with the world. Welcome to my blog, where I share my experiences, reflections, and the things that inspire me most.
